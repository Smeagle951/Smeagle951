## Overview

FortSmart is a **precision agriculture intelligence platform** designed to support agronomic decision-making across the entire crop lifecycle.

It enables structured data collection, geospatial tracking, and agronomic analysis — transforming field observations into reliable and actionable insights.

The platform is engineered to operate under real-world agricultural constraints, including unstable connectivity, large-scale operations, and high variability in field data.

---

## Core Capabilities

### Spatial Intelligence
- Plot-level (talhão) management with geospatial precision  
- Interactive field mapping and boundary control  
- GPS-based georeferenced data collection  

### Agronomic Intelligence
- Structured agronomic reporting engine  
- Phenological stage tracking  
- Historical analysis across crop cycles  

### Offline-First Infrastructure
- Fully operational without internet connectivity  
- Local database (SQLite) as source of truth  
- Intelligent synchronization with cloud backend  

### Data & Workflow Integration
- Standardized field data collection  
- Multi-user collaboration with access control  
- Export-ready reports (PDF, JSON, structured formats)  

### Security & Compliance
- Encrypted local storage  
- Audit trail of operations  
- LGPD-aligned data handling  

---

## Positioning

FortSmart is not just a field application.

It is a **data infrastructure layer for agriculture**, ensuring that every field interaction becomes structured, traceable, and decision-ready information.
